# Grade 10 Biology — Chapter 4: Photosynthesis

Photosynthesis is the process by which green plants convert light energy into
chemical energy stored in glucose.

## Key facts (for the AMAdocs end-to-end test)

- The overall word equation is: carbon dioxide + water -> glucose + oxygen.
- It takes place mainly in the **palisade mesophyll** cells of the leaf.
- The green pigment that absorbs light is called **chlorophyll**, found in
  organelles called **chloroplasts**.
- Photosynthesis has two stages: the **light-dependent reactions** (in the
  thylakoid membranes) and the **light-independent reactions** or Calvin cycle
  (in the stroma).
